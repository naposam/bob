FOUNDATION

VISSION

We envision a world of safety consciousness and individual motivation drive to develop opportunities.

MISSION

To empower communities and create individual and organization safety awareness to promote optimal health, good environment and human security through training and education .

SERVICES

  1. Health and Safety Advocacy
  2. Charitable Donations
  3. Skill and Career workshop
  4. Safety Consultancy Services (SCS)

VOLUNTEERS INFO

Adom Zoe Foundation has volunteers in several regions of the country including Greater Accra, Western Region and Ashanti Region..
